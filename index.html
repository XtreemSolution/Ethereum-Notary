No compatible Ethereum provider has been detected in your browser.
Fingerprinting a document onto the Ethereum blockchain requires a small payment in Ether (the gas price of a standard Ethereum transaction). To sign the transaction with your Ethereum key and approve the payment you need a Ethereum wallet application able to inject a compatible interface into your browser. We recommend the MetaMask plugin for this. Without a compatible wallet, you may still you this service, but files will be notarized onto the Rinkeby Ethereum test network, rather than the main blockchain network.
